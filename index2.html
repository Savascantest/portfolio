About Me

MSc in Smart EdTech | MA in Language Teaching

I am a language educator and educational technology professional with experience in digital learning, AI systems evaluation and annotation work, and learner-centered instructional design. My work combines practical teaching experience with academic training in learning sciences and educational technology.

With over eight years of experience teaching English and French in both classroom and online environments, I am particularly drawn to questions about how technology can be designed to meaningfully change what learners do, not just digitize existing instruction.

What defines my background is holding both a teaching qualification and an EdTech research degree simultaneously; I was designing lessons and studying learning systems at the same time. My areas of focus include adaptive learning, AI in education, instructional design, and digital pedagogy.

Currently based in Essen, Germany, I am open to opportunities in educational technology research, instructional design, AI-supported learning, learning experience design, and academic project development.
